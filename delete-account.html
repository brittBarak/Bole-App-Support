Delete Your Bole Account

If you wish to delete your Bole account and all associated data, you can do so directly from within the Bole app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Bole app.
  2. Go to your Profile section.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice.

Once you confirm, your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours. This is an irreversible action, and your data cannot be recovered after deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your account information (name, email, and profile details)
  • Any activity or content associated with your account

Some minimal, non-personal information (e.g., aggregated analytics) may be retained for up to 90 days for system integrity and legal compliance.
